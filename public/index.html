      How to play

      On your turn, draw glass shards from the kiln one at a time. Every shard is revealed to all.

      Crack! Draw a colour you already drew this turn and your glass shatters — you lose every shard you drew and your turn ends.

      Stop in time and you set each shard into your window. Same colours may never touch side-by-side. A prism is wild and may sit anywhere.

      Perfect Spectrum: survive 6 draws in one turn and score +7 on the spot.

      • +1 each shard set in your window
      • +3 socket filled with its matching colour
      • +4 completed row · +6 completed column
      • +7 Perfect Spectrum
      • +10 first to finish their window

      When someone completes their window, the round is played out so all have equal turns — then the brightest window wins.
